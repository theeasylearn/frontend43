About us

Welcome to Saurashtra Cricket Academy

A legacy of cricketing excellence since 1975. Founded by Ankit Patel in Bhavnagar, we have been a beacon for aspiring cricketers for nearly five decades. Located at 105, Eva Surbhi, Opposite Aksharwadi Temple, Waghwadi Road, Bhavnagar, our academy offers world-class facilities and expert coaching to nurture young talent.

Our facilities include professional grounds, net practice areas, and personalized coaching plans like Regular Coaching and One-to-One Coaching. With a team of experienced coaches and a structured approach to cricket training, we ensure every player receives the attention they need to thrive.

Join us to embark on a journey of growth, skill, and passion for cricket.

Where Passion Meets Precision, and Talent Becomes Triumph.
Vision

To be a leading cricket academy that identifies, nurtures, and develops raw talent into professional players capable of competing on the global stage. We strive to create an environment that fosters discipline, teamwork, and sportsmanship while promoting physical and mental well-being.

  • Offering world-class coaching, facilities, and resources tailored to the unique needs of each player.
  • Bridging the gap between grassroots cricket and professional opportunities through exposure to competitive matches and advanced training programs.
  • Instilling core values like resilience, focus, and leadership to prepare players for the challenges of professional cricket.
Mission
  • To provide world-class training and facilities to aspiring cricketers.
  • To instill discipline, teamwork, and sportsmanship in every player.
  • To bridge the gap between grassroots cricket and professional opportunities.
